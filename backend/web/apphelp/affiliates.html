Affiliates

How do I view details of Affiliates

1. Affiliates page

Click on the Affiliates icon to navigate to the Affiliates page

2. Affiliates details

You will be directed to a page where you can see a list of affiliated institutions

Click on an affiliated institution to see their corresponding details

You will be redirected to a page where you can see the affiliated institutions details

How do I search for an Affiliate

1. Affiliates page

Click on the Affiliates icon to navigate to the Affiliates page

2. Affiliates details

Search for an affiliated institution by using the search bar on top

Type in the name of the an Institution on the search box and click on the search button.
