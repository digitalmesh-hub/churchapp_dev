Settings

How do I change the default settings?

1. Settings page

Click on the Settings (gear) icon in the bottom of the page to navigate to the Settings page

2. Change your settings

You can change the settings as per the options provided here.

2. Logout

Click on the "Logout" button to log out of the application

How do I manage offline settings?

1. Settings page

Click on the Settings (gear) icon in the bottom of the page to navigate to the Settings page

2. Change offline settings

Click on the "Switch to offline mode" option from the listed settings

2. Settings Options

Turn "off" or "on" the required functionalities for offline mode and click "Sync Now". The settings will now take effect.

Click on "Clear offline data" to remove all data stored for offline use.
