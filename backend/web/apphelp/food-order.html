Food Ordering

How do I order food/drinks ?

1. Go to Food Ordering page

Click on the Food Order icon to navigate to the Food Ordering page

2. Food Category

There will be a list of food categories to choose from. Choose any one from the list to proceed to order a food item from the respective category.

3. Dishes

From food category, you will be directed to the food listing page, where all the available dishes are listed

Click on a dish to see its details

4. Food Detail

You can now see the details of the dish that you've selected and can order it by clicking on the "Add to Cart" button on the bottom of the page

You can all increase or decrease the number of the same dish you would like to order, by using the plus or minus buttons right above the Add to Cart button.

5. Cart

After a dish has been added to the cart, you can view your cart by clicking on the cart icon on the top right of the page

On the cart page, you can change the number of dishes you would like to order and even remove a dish from your cart.

Finally, click on the "Place Order" button on the bottom to place your order.

6. Previous Orders

You can view your previous orders and its details as well as the status of your order by clicking on the My Orders button.
