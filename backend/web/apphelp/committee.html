Committee

How do I see the committee members?

1. Committee page

Click on the committee icon to navigate to the committee page

2. Committee types

Click on a committee type from the tab on the top. Each tab represents each type of committee. Clicking on it lets you view the corresponding committee details

2. View previous years members

There are arrows, right below the committee type tabs for navigating through each year

Navigate forward or backwards through the years to see the committee members for the corresponding year.
