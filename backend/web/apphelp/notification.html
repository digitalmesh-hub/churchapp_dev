User Manual

Notificaton

How do I see my notifications ?

1. Go to Notification page

You can use the notification icon on the dashboard or the icon on the top bar to navigate to the notification page

2. Select the appropriate notification

There will be a list of notifications in the top and you can start by selecting any one of them.

How do I wish a member on their birthday ?

1. Go to notification page

Click on the birthday icon on the top of the page

You will see a list of members who have their birthdays on that particular day

There are two options for wishing a member. You can either "CALL" or send an "SMS" using the respective icons provided

Note that SMS and CALL Charges may apply

2. Member details

Clicking on a member will redirect you to a page where you can see their details

3. Send Wishes

After selecting the member details, there are two options for wishing a member, either you can "CALL" or send an "SMS"

Note that SMS and CALL Charges may apply

How do I wish a member for their Anniversary ?

1. Go to notification page

Click on the anniversary icon on the top of the page

You will get a list of members who have anniversary on that particular day

There are two options for wishing a member. You can either "CALL" or send an "SMS" using the respective icons provided

Note that SMS and CALL Charges may apply

2. Member details

Clicking on the member will redirect to a page where you can see their details

3. Send Wishes

On the members detail page, you either send wishes to the member or send wishes to the members spouse. Use the "CALL" or "SMS" icons to proceed.

Note that SMS and CALL Charges may apply

How do I view the details of an event ?

1. Go to notification page

Click on the events icon on the top of the page

You will see a list of Events

2.Event Deatils

Click on an event to see its details

3. RSVP for an event

Toggle the radio button next to the "Would you like to attend?" label to RSVP for an event

You can then select the number of members, guests and children who will be attending.

How do I view the news ?

1. Go to notification page

Click on the news icon on the top of the page

You will get a list of latest news

2.News Details

Click on a news to see its details
