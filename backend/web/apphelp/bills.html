User Manual

Bills

How do I view my bills ?

1. Go to Bills page

Click on the Bills icon to navigate to the Bills page

2. Monthly Bills

There are 2 arrows on both side of the date at the top. Click on the next or previous arrows to navigate through each months bill

How do I make Payments ?

1. Click Pay Now Button

Click on the Pay Now button in the bottom of bill page

2. Confirm Amount

Type the amount

Then click on the continue button to go to the next page

3. Review Payment

Click on Pay Now button to Complete Payment

4. Payment Confirmation Message

You are now on the Payment Status page. If the payment was failed, then please Click on Go Back button and try again

How do I export my bill ?

1. Click Export bill button

Click on the Export bill button in the bottom of bill page

2. Export bill

Type in your email address on the popup

Then click on the export button to send a copy of the bill to your email address
