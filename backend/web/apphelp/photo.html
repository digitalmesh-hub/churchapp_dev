User Manual

Photos

How do I view photos of an event?

1. Go to Photos page

Click on the Photos icon to navigate to the Photos page

2. Open Album

There will be a list of photo albums with an album title

Click on the photo album title to open

3. View Photos

You will navigate to another page where you can see photos in that album

Click on the photo which you would like to see

How do I add photos of an event?

1. Open Photo album

Click on the photo album title to open that album

2. Add photo

You will be redirected to a page where you can see all the album photos

Click on the green plus button named "Add photos" which is on the top left of listed photos

3. Add photos to album

Clicking on "Add Photo" will take you to a page where you can add a photo by choosing the file location and a caption for each photo

Use the green plus icon to add multiple images to the album

Click on the green tick button save the photo to the respective album
