Conversation

How do I start a new conversation ?

1. Go to Conversation page

You can use the Conversation icon on the dashboard or the icon on the top bar for navigating to Conversation page

2. Start new conversation

Click on "Start New Conversation" icon from the conversations page

3. New conversation

For starting a new conversation first you have to select a "Topic Subject"

After selecting it,type your conversation message in the message box

Then select to whom you would want to start conversation by selecting "send to" dropdown

Finally, click on the green tick icon to start the conversation

How do I reply to a conversation ?

1. Conversation details

Click on a conversation from the conversations page. This will open the conversation details page

2. Reply to a conversation

On the conversation details page, click on the reply button on the top right to reply to the respective conversation

Click on the close button on the top left to close this conversation

How do I view the members in a conversation ?

1. View members

In the conversation detail page click on the member icon on the right on the conversation heading

You will see the list of all members who are in the conversation
