User Manual

Affiliated Institutions

How do I add an affiliated institution?

1. Go to Affiliated Institutions page

Click on the "Affiliated Institutions" link from the main menu to navigate to its page

2. Upload Affiliated Institution Logo

Click on the "choose file" button and upload the logo of the Affiliated institution

3. Fill in the following fields

Modify or fill in the fields to be updated, then click on the "Save" button to confirm and save the modified data

Fields marked with astrix (*) are manditory

4. View the Affiliated institution added

After the details of an affiliated institution are added, it can be viewed on the table right below the form.

How do I edit an affiliated institution?

1. Go to Affiliated Institutions page

Click on the "Affiliated Institutions" link from the main menu to navigate to its page

2. Click on the Edit icon

On the Affiliates page, you can see the listing of all the affiliated institutions. Click on the edit icon next to the institution you would like to edit

3. Edit populated data

Upon clicking the edit icon, the details of that institution will be populated on to the fields above the listing. Click on the "Save" button to save the changes made

How do I delete an affiliated institution?

1. Go to Affiliated Institutions page

Click on the "Affiliated Institutions" link from the main menu to navigate to its page

2. Click on the Delete icon

On the Affiliates page, you can see the listing of all the affiliated institutions. Click on the delete icon next to the institution you would like to delete

3. Delete alert message

On clicking the delete icon, a popup message will appear requesting you to confirm delete. Click on "Okay" and the affiliated institution will now be deleted
