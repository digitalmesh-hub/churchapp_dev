User Manual

News

How do I add a news item?

1. News registration

Click on the "News" link on the main menu and click on the sub link "News registration"

2. Fill in the title and content

Type in the title of the news and the any text content describing the news as such using the rich text editor

3. News date

Select the date of the news and the expiry date of the news on the feilds provided and click the save button.
You will now have created a news item

4. Publish News

Go back to the news listing page and click on "Publish" to push the news into the app. Else, the news will only apprear on the news date specified while creating the news item.

How do I search for a news item?

1. Go to News page

Click on the News icon from the dashboard to navigate to the News listing page

2. Search News Listing

Search for a news item by title on the text field provided on top

How do I remove a news item?

1. Delete News

Click on the "Delete" button the news listing page to remove any news item.
