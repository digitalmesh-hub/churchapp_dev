How do I edit details of a staff member?

1. Go to Staff registration page

Click on the "Staff Registration" link on the main menu to head to the staff registration screen

2. Staff registration

Fill in the fields provided in the "Staff/Spouse" tab and click the "Next" button

3. Staff settings

Select the address for communications and check mark the notifications you would like to receive and click save. Now you have successfully registered a staff member

How do I find a staff member?

1. Go to Staff listing page

Click on the "Staff Listing" link on the main menu to head to the staff listing screen

2. Staff listing

This page will automatically display all staff member profiles. You can search using a staff membership number, staff name or phone number.
