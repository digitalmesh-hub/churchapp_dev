User Manual

Photo Album

How do I create a new photo album?

1. Go to photo album page

Click on the photo album icon to navigate to the respective page

2. Create an album

Click on the "Create Album" button on the photo album tab to navigate to the create album page

3. Add image

Click on the plus icon on the box and you will see a popup where you can add an image and its caption

3. Upload the image

Choose an image using the file browser on the popup, then type in a caption and click save

4. Publish

Once an image has been added, you will have the option to either publish this album or keep adding more images to it. Click on publish to push this album into the photo albums page

How do I edit an album ?

1. VIew album

To edit an album, go to the photo album page and click on the "View Album" button on the album you need to edit

2. Edit album

Click on the edit button to edit the caption of that image. The delete button removes that image from the album. Clicking on the "Make album cover" button will make that image the cover photo for that album.
Finally, clicking on the "Publish" button will save the changes made into the album

How do I delete an album ?

1. Delete an album

Click on the delete button to remove the entire album from the application

How do I approve the photos pending in an album ?

1. View pending photos

Click on the "Photos pending approval" tab and you can see the albums into which new photos have been sent for approval. Then click on the "View" button

2. Approve photos

Click on the checkboxes to approve each photo and click on "Add to album"
