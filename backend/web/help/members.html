User Manual

Members

How do I add details of a new member?

1. Member Registration link

Click on the "Member Registration" link from the main menu to open the Member Registration page

2. Upload member photo

On the "Member/Spouse" tab, click on "Choose file/browse" to upload the photo of the member

3. Use this form to update details of individual members

If a member does not wish for his mobile number to be displayed in the app's directory listing, you can check the "Keep number private" option.

Fields marked with astrix (*) are manditory

4. Phone numbers

Please update the number as +Country Code Area Code Phone number

5. Fill in the dependant details

Type in the dependant (Child) details of the member and click "Add"

6. Click next

After adding a dependant, click next to move to the settings page

7. Notification Settings

All communications to a member can be directed to either their business or residential address by selecting the appropriate option from the "Communication Address" drop down.
If a member does not wish to receive notifications from the institution, click on the appropriate check boxes in "Settings".

How do I add / change the photograph of a member?

1. Member Listing

Click on the Member link on the main menu and select the Member listing link

2. Manage Member

Click on the Manage icon on the Member listing page

3. Choose a Photo

Click on either "Remove" to remove the current photo, or click on "Choose file/browse" to upload a new photo

4. Open photo

Choose a photo from the file browser and click on "Open".
Now the photo selected will appear in the preview.

5. Next Button

Click the next button on both the "Member/Spouse" tab as well as the following "Dependants" tab

6. Save Button

Finally click "Save" on the Settings tab to finalize your action

How do I search for a member?

1. Go to Members page

Click on the Member icon to navigate to the Member page

2. Search Member Listing

Search for a member by name on the text field provided on top

How do I edit details of an existing member?

1. Member list link

Click on the "Member Listing" link from the main menu to open the Member listing page

2. Manage Member

Choose the member whoes details you need to edit and click on the "Manage" button

3. Edit member details

You can edit all the details of a member here, including business and residential addresses.

Similarly the details of the spouse, dependants and assistant can also be edited by clicking on the "Next" button on this page

4. Save

You must click on the "Save" button on the settings tab in order for the changes to be made effective.

Once the save button is clicked, you will have successfully edited the member profile

How do I delete a members’ details?

1. Member Listing

Click on the "Member Listing" link from the main menu to open the Member listing page

2. Delete Button

Click on the "Delete" button against the member you want to delete

3. Alert Message

You will see an alert message as a warning before the member is deleted. Click "Okay" to permanently delete the member details.

You have now successfully deleted a member

How do I approve a pending request to update a member’s information?

1. Member list link

Click on the "Member Listing" link from the main menu to open the Member listing page

2. Pending approval tab

Click on the "Pending Approval" tab beside the "Member List" tab to view all the members with pending requests

3. View Changes

Click on "View Changes" button to see the data pending approval

4. Fields with data pending approval

All fields that have been edited by the member will be highlighted with a red border as well as info .
Clicking on the will display the new data and also give you an option to approve or reject the request.

5. Approve or reject

Click on "Approve" or "Reject" on the change requested

6. Save

Click on the "Save" button at the end of the page to save the approval/rejection made.

7. Alert Message

A message will appear saying that you have succefully made the changes. Click "Okay" and you have now successfully approved a pending request to update a member data
