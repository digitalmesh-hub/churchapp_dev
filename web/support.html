About the App

Re-member Church is a mobile application designed for church communities. Only registered members of a church can access and use the app.

Church administrators manage member data through a secure web portal. Members can view information from the member directory and receive notifications such as birthdays and anniversaries.

Need Help?

If you face any issues with login, access, or data displayed in the app, please contact your church administrator first.

Technical Support:

Email: info@thateurekamoment.com

Payments & Subscriptions

Re-member Church does not offer any paid services, subscriptions, or in-app purchases. The app does not process any kind of payments.

Privacy

Member information is managed only by authorized church administrators. Data is used strictly for internal church communication purposes and is not shared with third parties.
